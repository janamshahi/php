about spc collage

Sudur Paschimanchal Campus (SPC) is an unbeatable center of academic excellence for world-class education in management, arts, and education.
It aims to transform each graduate with skill competence, ability, willingness, and professional maturity to face the challenges and also to take benefit of the opportunities of a globally competitive business environment.
More importantly, SPC strives for professional development to meet the ever-increasing needs of business communities, development agencies, and social and public-sector organizations.

About Us- BIM-TU

The Bachelor of Information Management (BIM) program at SPC aims to equip students with the technical and managerial skills necessary to pursue careers in the field of Information Technology. The program offers a comprehensive curriculum that covers a range of topics including programming languages, database management, system analysis, and design, software engineering, web development, and e-commerce.
